**Empowering South African Youth Through Political & Economic Education**

> Created by **Bongiwe Mokhati** | Computer Science Student @ Tshwane University of Technology

---

## 👩🏾‍💻 About the Creator

**YouthPolitics is my original idea.** My name is Bongiwe Mokhati, and I am a Computer Science student at Tshwane University of Technology. I created this platform to solve a problem I personally experienced: wanting to understand politics and economics, but finding most information too complex or inaccessible for young people.

This isn't just a website — it's proof that young women in tech can create solutions that empower an entire generation.

**My vision:** A generation of informed young South Africans who have the knowledge and confidence to engage in discussions, make informed decisions, and shape the future of our country.

---

## 📖 About the Platform

YouthPolitics is a free, interactive educational platform designed to help young South Africans understand:

- 🇿🇦 **South African Politics** - Parties, elections, history, and leaders
- 🌍 **World Politics** - Global events and their impact on SA
- 💰 **Economy & Finance** - Financial literacy, investing, and economic concepts
- 📰 **News Explained** - Current events broken down in simple language
- 📚 **History** - Apartheid, liberation struggle, and Black Consciousness

---

## ✨ Features

### 🤖 AI Assistant
Ask questions about any political or economic topic in plain language. The AI explains complex concepts simply.

### 👥 Political Leaders
Learn about key figures in SA politics - from Nelson Mandela and Winnie Madikizela-Mandela to current leaders like Cyril Ramaphosa and Julius Malema.

### 📊 Live Statistics
Up-to-date data on:
- Youth unemployment (59.7%)
- Inflation (5.3%)
- GDP growth (1.2%)
- And more (June 2026)

### 🎮 Gamification
- Interactive quizzes
- Achievement badges
- Progress tracking
- Swipeable educational cards

### 📱 Mobile-Friendly
Works perfectly on phones, tablets, and computers.
