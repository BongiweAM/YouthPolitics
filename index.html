What is Inflation? (June 2026)

When prices go up over time, your R100 buys less than it did last year. SA inflation is 5.3% right now — that means if bread cost R20 last year, it costs R21.06 now. Good news: it's DOWN from 5.9% last year.

🤝

What is the GNU?

After the 2024 elections no party won alone. The ANC (40.2%), DA (21.8%) and 5 other parties formed the Government of National Unity (GNU) to run the country together.

SA in BRICS — Why It Matters

BRICS is a group of powerful countries: Brazil, Russia, India, China, South Africa. Being in BRICS means SA can access loans, trade deals and investment from these giants.

Apartheid ended in 1994 but its economic effects remain. The wealth gap between black and white South Africans is still one of the widest in the world (Gini 0.63).

Your Money. Your Power. 💸

Most schools don't teach you this. We do.

01

Start Saving Now

Even R50/month grows. Put it in a tax-free savings account (TFSA). After 10 years at 10% interest, R500/month becomes over R100,000.

02

Property Investment

You can buy property with no deposit using a 100% bond. Rental income can pay your bond. Property value grows with inflation. Start learning now.

03

Invest in the Stock Market

ETFs on EasyEquities let you own small pieces of companies like Amazon, Naspers, and Sasol. Start with R1. Your money grows while you sleep.

04

Skills = Income

Coding, digital marketing, video editing, carpentry — any skill you master can make you money. Freelance platforms like Upwork pay in USD.

Know Your Roots

The History You Need to Know 📖

1960

Sharpeville Massacre

69 black protesters killed by apartheid police. This moment turned the ANC to armed resistance and shocked the world. The ANC was banned.

1976

Soweto Uprising

Students — YOUR age — rose up against Bantu Education. Hector Pieterson was shot dead. This moment changed South Africa forever.

1994

Freedom Day — First Democratic Election

For the first time, all South Africans could vote. Nelson Mandela became president. But economic inequality was left largely intact.

2015

#FeesMustFall

Students shut down universities demanding free education. Government froze fee increases. The movement showed the power of youth activism.

2024

ANC Loses Majority — GNU Formed

The ANC received only 40.2% of votes — first time below 50% since 1994. A coalition government was formed. A new era of South African politics began.
