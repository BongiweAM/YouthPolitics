Timeline

Key Events 2024–2026 📅

May 2024

National Elections — ANC Below 50%

South Africa's most significant election since 1994. ANC gets 40.2%, forms Government of National Unity with 6 other parties. DA's Steenhuisen joins Cabinet for the first time.

Jan 2024

SA Takes Israel to ICJ for Genocide

SA files a case at the International Court of Justice alleging Israel is committing genocide in Gaza. ICJ issues provisional measures ordering Israel to prevent genocidal acts. SA gains global attention.

2023

Worst Load Shedding Year in SA History

South Africa experienced over 200 days of load shedding in 2023, with stage 6 blackouts becoming routine. Estimated R400bn economic damage. Eskom CEO fired. Energy crisis reaches breaking point.

2022

Zondo Commission Final Report

The Zondo Commission releases its final report on State Capture. Names hundreds of individuals involved in looting the state. Recommends prosecutions. Most implicated individuals still face no criminal charges by 2026.

2015

#FeesMustFall Movement

University students across SA shut down campuses demanding free education. The movement leads to zero percent fee increases for 2016 and eventually expanded NSFAS funding. A defining moment in post-1994 youth politics.
